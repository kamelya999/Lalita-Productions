About Lalita Productions

Lalita Productions is a self-run production company established in 2009 with the purpose to produce shows in the independent music scene, expose emerging bands and artists, and bring together creative collectives to reach common goals. LP has been through many stages and functions, from producing music and albums, fundraising, and community outreach, to recently booking and representing bands, producing indie music festivals and music videos. Presently there is constant work offers flowing into the company as we manage bands such as Los Bronson, Sr. Langosta, Los Cerezos, La Banda Municipal de Makula Barun, among others. As the company keeps growing, there are legal matters that need responding to, such as permits, licenses, contracts and others. Getting the proper paperwork done is one of the main challenges of LP at this moment, apart from the growing need for new equipment and financing of our latest musical releases. In the last year Lalita Productions has hosted over 10 events, produced Bronson Fest, 2 music videos and 2 albums. We have already started working on our 2016 projects which include 3 albums, 2 videos, and the second year of Bronson Fest. Lalita Productions is committed to creating and promoting quality entertainment, as a means to support local economy, enrich our community and give much needed support to the indie music scene in Puerto Rico.
