About Lalita Productions

Lalita Productions is an independent production company established in 2009 with the purpose to produce shows in the underground / independent music scene, exposing emerging bands and artists, and bringing together creative collectives to reach common goals. LP has been through many stages and functions, from producing music and albums, fundraising, and community outreach, to recently booking and representing bands, producing indie music festivals, as well as music videos.
